Teeth Whitening

What is Teeth Whitening?
Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental treatment designed to remove stains and discoloration, restoring the natural brightness of your teeth. It’s a safe and effective procedure performed by dental professionals using advanced whitening agents.

Why do you need Teeth Whitening?
Over time, teeth can become stained due to coffee, tea, smoking, red wine, medications, or aging. Whitening removes these stains and improves confidence, appearance, and smile aesthetics.

Advantages:

  • Instant smile improvement
  • Boosts confidence and appearance
  • Painless and non-invasive
  • Safe under professional supervision
  • Long-lasting results with proper care

When should you do it?

  • If teeth look yellow or stained
  • Before weddings, interviews, events
  • After professional cleaning
  • Every 6–12 months depending on habits

How we do it:
We assess your teeth and gums, protect sensitive areas, and apply professional whitening gel. Treatment takes about 45–90 minutes and results are visible immediately.

Aftercare Tips:

  • Avoid staining foods for 48 hours
  • Maintain brushing and flossing routine
  • Use whitening toothpaste
  • Attend routine dental checkups
