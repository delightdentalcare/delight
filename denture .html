What are Dentures?

Dentures are high-quality, custom-made artificial teeth designed to replace missing teeth. Whether you have lost a few teeth or all of them, our dentures restore your **chewing ability, speech clarity, and facial structure.**

At Delight Dental Care, we don't just give you teeth; we give you back your lifestyle. Our dentures are crafted to fit securely so you never have to worry about them slipping while you talk or eat.

The Danger of Delaying

When teeth are missing, your jawbone begins to shrink. This leads to:

  • ❌ Collapsed facial appearance (aging you faster)
  • ❌ Shifting of remaining teeth
  • ❌ Severe digestive issues from poor chewing

Replacing teeth early prevents jawbone shrinkage!

Professional Care Instructions

Daily Cleaning

Use a soft denture brush to remove food and plaque daily.

Rest Your Gums

Always remove dentures at night to allow gum tissues to breathe.

Hydration

Soak in a denture solution overnight to prevent them from warping.

⚠️ If your dentures hurt, slip, or cause sores, visit us immediately for an adjustment!
