Your gums are the foundation of your teeth. We treat infections at the root to ensure your smile lasts a lifetime.

The Silent Threat: Gum Disease

Gum disease often starts without pain. It begins with plaque buildup that irritates the gums, eventually leading to bone loss and loose teeth.

[attachment_0](attachment)

Warning Signs:

  • • Bleeding while brushing or flossing
  • • Swollen, red, or tender gums
  • • Persistent bad breath
  • • Receding gum line (teeth look longer)
