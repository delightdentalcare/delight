When is an Extraction Necessary?

At Delight Dental Care, our first goal is always to save your natural teeth. However, sometimes removing a tooth is the best way to protect your overall health and stop unbearable pain.

✔

🚨 Emergency Tooth Pain?

If you are experiencing severe swelling, fever, or pain that keeps you awake at night, you may have an infection. Delaying treatment can be dangerous.

What to Expect

1. Complete Numbing

We use high-quality local anesthesia. You will feel pressure, but **zero pain.** We don't start until you are ready.

2. Quick Procedure

Most extractions take only 15–30 minutes. Our doctors use gentle techniques to minimize trauma to the area.

3. Rapid Healing

We provide you with clear aftercare steps and a follow-up plan to ensure you heal perfectly and quickly.

Post-Extraction Care

  • 🦷 Bite on Gauze: Keep firm pressure for 30-45 mins.
  • 🦷 Avoid Straws: Do not suck through a straw for 24 hours.
  • 🦷 Soft Foods: Eat yogurt, pap, or soup for the first day.
  • 🦷 Rest: Avoid heavy lifting or exercise for 24 hours.
  • 🦷 No Smoking: Avoid smoking to prevent "Dry Socket."
  • 🦷 Ice Pack: Use an ice pack to reduce any swelling.
